Our Comprehensive Services

Each service is designed to provide specific, targeted care for your health needs.

Virtual Primary Care

Ongoing comprehensive primary care from the comfort of your home with consistent provider relationships. Your medical home for routine and preventive healthcare needs.

What's Included:

  • Annual wellness visits and consultations
  • Acute illness management (colds, flu, infections)
  • Health risk assessments and screenings
  • Care coordination and health advocacy
  • Follow-up visits and ongoing support

Please Note:

  • Does not include emergency care (call 911 for emergencies)
  • Does not replace in-person physical exams when required
  • Limited controlled substance prescriptions

Chronic Disease Management

Personalized support and management for chronic conditions with regular monitoring, medication adjustments, and lifestyle guidance to optimize your health outcomes.

Conditions We Manage:

  • Hypertension (High Blood Pressure)
  • Type 2 Diabetes and Pre-diabetes
  • High Cholesterol & Lipid Disorders
  • Thyroid Conditions (Hypothyroidism)
  • Asthma & COPD Management
  • Obesity and Weight Management

Please Note:

  • Complex cases may require specialist referral
  • Does not include acute emergency treatment
  • Regular monitoring required for optimal management

Preventive & Lifestyle Care

Proactive healthcare focused on prevention, healthy habits, and long-term wellness through personalized guidance and evidence-based lifestyle interventions.

What's Included:

  • Personalized wellness planning
  • Nutrition and exercise guidance
  • Preventive health screenings
  • Stress management techniques
  • Sleep optimization strategies
