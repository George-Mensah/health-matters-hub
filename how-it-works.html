Step-by-Step Guide

Here's exactly what to expect at every stage of your virtual care journey.

1

Schedule Your Appointment

5 minutes

Choose a convenient time that fits your schedule using our secure online booking system. We offer appointments during business hours, evenings, and weekends.

What You'll Do:

  • Select appointment type (new patient, follow-up, chronic care)
  • Choose preferred date and time
  • Complete brief intake form
  • Provide insurance information (if applicable)
  • Receive confirmation email with instructions

What You'll Need:

  • Computer, tablet, or smartphone
  • Stable internet connection
  • Valid email address
  • Photo ID (for new patients)
  • List of current medications
2

Prepare for Your Visit

10-15 minutes

Get everything ready to make the most of your virtual consultation. Preparation helps ensure efficient, productive visits.

Before Your Visit:

  • Test your camera and microphone
  • Find a quiet, private space
  • Have your medications ready
  • Note down symptoms and questions
  • Take any relevant vitals (if available)

You'll Receive:

  • Secure video link 24 hours before
  • Reminder text/email 2 hours before
  • Pre-visit questionnaire
  • Instructions for virtual waiting room
  • Technical support contact
3

Connect with Your Provider

30-60 minutes

Meet face-to-face with your board-certified provider through our secure, HIPAA-compliant video platform for a comprehensive consultation.

During Your Visit:

  • Review medical history and concerns
  • Discuss symptoms and lifestyle factors
  • Receive personalized assessment
  • Collaborate on treatment plan
  • Ask questions and get answers

Virtual Visit Features:

  • End-to-end encryption
  • Screen sharing for education
  • Digital prescription capability
  • Secure document sharing
  • Family member participation option
4

Receive Your Care Plan

Immediate to 24 hours

Get your personalized treatment plan, prescriptions, and follow-up instructions through our secure patient portal.

After Your Visit:

  • Personalized treatment summary
  • Prescriptions sent to your pharmacy
  • Lab/imaging orders if needed
  • Specialist referrals when necessary
  • Educational resources

Next Steps May Include:

  • Scheduling follow-up appointment
  • Completing lab tests
  • Starting new medications
  • Implementing lifestyle changes
  • Connecting with specialists
5

Ongoing Support & Follow-up

Continuous

Maintain continuous communication with your care team through secure messaging and scheduled follow-ups to ensure optimal health outcomes.

Ongoing Support:

  • Secure messaging with care team
  • Medication refill requests
  • Lab result explanations
  • Symptom check-ins
  • Health goal tracking

Long-term Care:

  • Regular chronic condition monitoring
  • Preventive care scheduling
  • Annual wellness visits
  • Medication adjustments as needed
  • Health progress reviews

Labs, Imaging & Referrals

When diagnostic testing is needed, we coordinate everything seamlessly:

  • Lab orders at convenient local facilities
  • Imaging referrals (X-ray, MRI, CT, ultrasound)
  • Specialist referrals with care coordination
  • Result follow-up and explanation
  • Insurance pre-authorization assistance

Insurance & Payment

Transparent pricing and flexible payment options:

  • Direct billing to most major insurance plans
  • Clear self-pay pricing for uninsured patients
  • FSA/HSA eligible services
  • Cost estimates before service
  • Flexible payment plans available

Note: Insurance coverage varies by plan. We verify benefits before your first visit.

Technical Requirements

What you need for a successful virtual visit:

  • Smartphone, tablet, or computer
  • Stable internet connection
  • Webcam and microphone
  • Updated web browser (Chrome, Safari, Edge)
  • Private, well-lit space

Technical support available 30 minutes before your appointment

Common Questions

Quick answers to frequently asked questions about virtual care.

Is virtual care as effective as in-person visits?

For many primary care needs, yes. Virtual care is particularly effective for medication management, chronic condition follow-ups, and health consultations. Some conditions may require in-person evaluation.

What if I need prescriptions?

We can prescribe most non-controlled medications electronically to your preferred pharmacy. Controlled substances have specific regulations and limitations.

Can I use my insurance?

We accept most major insurance plans. Our team verifies benefits before your first visit and provides clear cost information.

What if I have technical issues?

Technical support is available before and during your appointment. We can also switch to phone consultation if video isn't working.
