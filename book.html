Booking Questions

Common questions about scheduling virtual visits

How far in advance can I book?

Appointments can be scheduled up to 90 days in advance. Same-day appointments may be available based on provider schedule.

What if I need to cancel or reschedule?

You can reschedule or cancel up to 24 hours before your appointment without charge. Late cancellations may incur a fee.

Do you accept insurance?

Yes, we accept most major insurance plans. We'll verify your benefits and contact you if there are coverage questions.

What technology do I need?

You'll need a smartphone, tablet, or computer with camera and microphone, plus a stable internet connection.

Can family members join the visit?

Yes, family members can join the video call. Please let us know in advance if multiple people will be attending.

What if I have technical issues?

Technical support is available 30 minutes before your appointment. We can also switch to a phone call if needed.
